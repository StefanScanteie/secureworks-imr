Introduction

Incident Management Retainer (IMR) services from Secureworks help organizations prepare for, respond to, and recover from cybersecurity incidents. This questionnaire is designed to guide prospects in identifying their needs without requiring deep knowledge of each service. By answering these questions, you will receive tailored recommendations from the IMR service catalog, ensuring your organization is better prepared and resilient against threats.
